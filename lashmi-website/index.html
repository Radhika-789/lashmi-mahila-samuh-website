Our Story

Since 2009, Lashmi Mahila Samuh has been more than just an organization; it is a heartbeat for women in Jaipur seeking strength and dignity. What began as a vision to provide sustainable livelihoods in underserved areas has blossomed into a thriving community of artisans. We believe that every handmade bag, every gift box, and every piece of stationery carries the resilience of a woman who has turned financial dependence into artistic ownership. Even through the quietest days of the pandemic, our spirit never wavered-we rebuilt, stronger than ever, proving that when a woman gains a skill, she doesn't just change her own life; she transforms her entire community. When you choose our eco-friendly paper crafts, you aren’t just buying a product—you are holding a story of hope, courage, and a promise of a brighter future.
