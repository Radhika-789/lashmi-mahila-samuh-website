Our Story

Where It All Began

Our journey started in 2018 with a simple vision: to create meaningful employment opportunities for underprivileged communities while promoting sustainable, eco-friendly products.

What began as a small workshop with five artisans has grown into a thriving community of over 50 skilled craftspeople, each bringing their unique talents and stories to life through their creations.

Every product we create carries the spirit of empowerment, sustainability, and hope. We believe in fair wages, safe working conditions, and nurturing the traditional crafts that make our culture rich.
